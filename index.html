Zenith

Young professionals are seeking personal fulfillment in the workplace and want to feel like the 40 hour work-week is helping them work towards a life they can truly enjoy instead of working them to death. Job fulfillment is not a one-size-fits all; job seekers want their careers to create a positive impact in the world while also reaching personal milestones that align with their interests and vision for their future. There is a need for an app for young professionals at any stage in their career to explore opportunities for job growth and fulfillment and learn about various career paths that appeal to their unique wants.
