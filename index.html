Zenith

Zenith is an easy to use solution that gets job seekers into the right career, to help them build a life they can truly enjoy. Job fulfillment is not a one-size-fits all; job seekers want their careers to create a positive impact in the world while also reaching personal milestones that align with their interests and vision for their future.

No matter if users are finding their first job, the dream position, or pivoting careers Zenith was created to help guide them to the next step. Our goal was to design a mobile solution that provided users with beginning to end resources to find them the next best thing in their careers.

Our Approach

Through thorough user interviews and surveys we learned that users sought careers that developed personal and professional growth. By reflecting on their past experience and future aspirations, we identified the need for reaching personal and professional goals, creating positive change, and aligning their interest with their career.

Problem Statement

We have observed that young professionals want careers that appeal to their interests, nurture growth in personal skills, and create an impact in the world. This is causing many to find interest in career exploration and new opportunities. How might we bridge the knowledge gap between their current journey and their definition of a successful career so that they can find fulfillment in future job opportunities?
